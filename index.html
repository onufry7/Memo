Instruction:

Classic memory game - Choose a fairy tale and find similar or identical pairs of pictures.

If the images are the same (Identical), they form pairs with identical images. You need to find images that look exactly the same. Otherwise, if they are similar (Similar), you need to find images that depict the same character but may be in different poses. They must always have a similar age. It may happen that one character will be depicted as a child, and the other as an adult. In such a situation, these are two different pairs; you need to find a pair for the adult and one for the child.
